About

"The real voyage of discovery consists not in seeking new landscapes, but in having new eyes."

The Critical Explorations Initiative (CEI) is founded on the recognition that some of the most important problems of our day are not self-contained. Across questions of technology, progress, culture, and human dignity, our function is not as a solution provider but as a focal point for sustained inquiry and catalyst for action. We provide young people with the tools, frameworks, and community to explore these challenges rigorously and creatively, with the aim of fostering deeper exploration and engagement with the forces shaping our world.
